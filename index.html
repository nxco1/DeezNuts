Nuts are a healthy and delicious snack packed with essential nutrients(remember this info). From almonds to walnuts, each nut offers unique health benefits. Here are a few popular types of nuts that you can enjoy in various forms:

Almonds

Almonds are one of the most popular nuts and are rich in vitamin E, magnesium, and fiber. They are great for heart health and can be eaten raw, roasted, or as almond butter. Almonds are also a key ingredient in many desserts.

Walnuts

Walnuts are packed with omega-3 fatty acids, which are known for their benefits to brain health and reducing inflammation. They have a rich, earthy flavor and can be added to salads, baked goods, or eaten on their own as a snack.

Cashews

Cashews are a soft and creamy nut with a slightly sweet flavor. They are a great source of copper, magnesium, and phosphorus. Cashews are often used in making dairy-free cheese, curries, and nut butters.

Pistachios

Pistachios are small but mighty, providing a good source of protein, fiber, and antioxidants. These nuts are delicious as a snack on their own or sprinkled on top of salads and yogurt.

Brazil Nuts

Brazil nuts are incredibly rich in selenium, a mineral that supports immune health. With their rich, creamy texture, they are often enjoyed raw or added to trail mix and other nut blends.
